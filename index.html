About

Ivan was introduced to photography through working in large commercial studios — building sets, shaping light, learning the craft from the inside out. That foundation led to years of commissioned work for luxury brands and advertising agencies.

After years of commercial work, a personal style emerged — analog film, warm light, the unhurried quality of a summer afternoon. A visual language built around female beauty, natural environments, and the kind of stillness that only exists between moments.

That world between discipline and instinct is where the work lives now.
