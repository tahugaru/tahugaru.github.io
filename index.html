Ivan Codrean

Ivan was introduced to photography through working in large commercial studios — building sets, shaping light, learning the craft from the inside out. That foundation led to commissioned work for luxury brands and advertising agencies.

After years of commercial work a personal style surfaced — filmic warmth, the slow hush of a summer afternoon, images of female beauty, nature, and the stillness between moments.
